This is a small web app de­signed to sup­port bell­ringers with learn­ing the ‘Stand­ard 41’ Sur­prise Minor meth­ods.

Table is a click­able table that shows the meth­ods ar­ranged in columns and rows de­pend­ing on their over / un­der work.

Quiz gen­er­ates ques­tions to test your know­ledge of the struc­ture of each method.

Prac­tice al­lows you to ‘tap-along’ to test your know­ledge of the blueline of a par­tic­u­lar method, or ran­dom leads.

Use Set­tings to en­able / dis­able cer­tain meth­ods from ap­pear­ing in the other tabs if you are learn­ing a sub­set of the 41.

If you would like the con­tent on this page to be big­ger then either use Ctrl+ & Ctrl- or pinch-to-zoom, whichever your device sup­ports.

The be­low table ex­ists in a few guises on the in­ter­net. It shows all 41 meth­ods laid out in a grid with those in the same column hav­ing the same work over the treble, and those in the same row hav­ing the same work un­der. Meth­ods that dif­fer only at the half-lead or lead-end are in ad­ja­cent columns/​rows.

Click a method name to show the blue line.
